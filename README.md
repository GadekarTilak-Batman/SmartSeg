# SmartSeg
SmartSeg Segregation is a highly polished, interactive full-stack civic hygiene and sustainability platform designed to assist and incentivize citizens in their waste management and waste segregation efforts under the Swachh Bharat initiatives.
The application provides the following core modules:
Multilingual AI Waste Mentor (Segregation Mitra Chat): A real-time, interactive assistant supporting multiple languages (English, Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Marathi, and Kannada). Users can type questions, try interactive quick questions, or utilize the computer vision dropzone (Swachh Camera Neural Analyzer) to classify waste materials instantly. It offers standard guidelines for disposing of materials in standard green (wet), blue (dry), and red (hazardous) buckets, along with text-to-speech voice playback support.
Swachh Rewards Hub & Milestones: A gamified ledger tracking Green Points (GP), daily Habit Segregation Streaks, and interactive Eco-Coach Mission lists. It features an adjustable Carbon Offset Slider Estimator calculating CO₂ reduction from recycling efforts, a portfolio of unlockable Eco Badges, and a community-wide Civil Leaderboard (e.g. for the Basavanagudi neighborhood) to boost clean-habit competition.
Grievance Complaint Reporting: A neighborhood ticketing system allowing citizens to report roadside dumps or municipal issues, tracking status updates in real-time.
Smart Awareness Quizzes: Interactive Swachh-themed trivia and interactive educational resources aiding users to better master clean- India compliance checklists.
